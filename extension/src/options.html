Twitch Mutual Follows

Extension Settings

Your Twitch Login

Enter your Twitch login to display mutual follows. The extension will try to detect your login automatically, but you can set it manually.

User Card Type

Choose which card to display mutual follows in. Native card is used by default.

Cache Management

The extension caches follow lists for 12 hours to speed up performance. You can clear the cache to refresh data.

How to Use?
1. Open any chat on Twitch
2. Click on a username in chat
3. You'll see a "Mutual Follows" section in the card
4. Click on the avatars to see the full list
